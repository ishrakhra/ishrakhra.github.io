Apple Cinnamon Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional, adjust for sweetness)
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • (add more if you love cinnamon!)...
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • A pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup milk (or a dairy-free alternative)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp melted butter (or oil)
  • 1 apple, peeled and grated
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Mix the Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, ground cinnamon, and salt.
  2. Prepare the Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk the milk, egg, vanilla extract, and melted butter together until smooth.
  3. Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir gently until just combined. Don‚Äôt overmix! Fold in the grated apple.
  4. Cook the Pancakes: Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or oil. Pour 1/4 cup of the batter for each pancake onto the skillet. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, about 2-3 minutes, then flip and cook for another 2-3 minutes or until golden brown.
  5. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the pancakes warm with a drizzle of maple syrup, a sprinkle of extra cinnamon, or some whipped cream. You can also top with extra apple slices or nuts for added texture!

Beetroot Juice

Ingredients

  • 2 medium-sized beetroots, peeled and chopped
  • 1 apple, cored and chopped (for sweetness)
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, peeled
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced...
  • 1-2 cups water (adjust for desired consistency)
  • Optional: A pinch of salt or black pepper
  • Optional: Mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the chopped beetroots, apple, ginger, and lemon juice.
  2. Add 1 cup of water and blend until smooth. If needed, add more water to achieve your desired consistency.
  3. Strain the mixture through a fine mesh sieve or cheesecloth into a glass or pitcher to remove the pulp, if desired.
  4. Stir in a pinch of salt or black pepper for added flavor, if using.
  5. Serve chilled or over ice, garnished with mint leaves if desired.

Spinach Parmesan Pasta

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta (penne or spaghetti)
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced...
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (or milk for a lighter option)
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the chopped spinach and cook until wilted, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the heavy cream. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet, along with the reserved pasta water. Toss to combine.
  6. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and mix until the cheese is melted and the pasta is coated.
  7. Season with red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and black pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil and additional Parmesan cheese if desired.

Chicken Veggie Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) boneless, skinless chicken breast, diced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup carrots, diced
  • 1 cup celery, diced...
  • 1 cup potatoes, diced
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, saut√©ing until fragrant and translucent.
  2. Add the diced chicken to the pot, cooking until it's no longer pink, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Stir in the carrots, celery, and potatoes. Cook for an additional 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Pour in the chicken broth and add the green beans, thyme, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for 25-30 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and the chicken is fully cooked.
  6. Adjust seasoning if necessary. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Lemon Fish Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g fish fillets (such as cod, tilapia, or your choice)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder (adjust for spice level)...
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1 green chili, slit (optional)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, marinate the fish fillets with lemon juice, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Set aside for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and green chili to the pan. Saut√© for a couple of minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add coriander powder and cumin powder. Stir well and cook for another minute.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk, stirring to combine. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.
  6. Carefully add the marinated fish fillets to the curry. Cook for about 10-15 minutes or until the fish is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  7. Adjust seasoning with salt and add more lemon juice if desired.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice or bread of your choice.

Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes...
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, stirring frequently.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, vegetable broth, sugar, dried basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat and let the soup simmer for 20-25 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  5. If using, stir in the heavy cream and heat through.
  6. Using an immersion blender, blend the soup until smooth (or transfer to a blender in batches). Return to the pot to heat if necessary.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves.

Banana Pecan Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt...
  • 3/4 cup sugar (adjust for sweetness)
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup chopped pecans (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). Grease a 4x8 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until smooth.
  3. Stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas.
  4. Add in the baking soda and salt, and mix well.
  5. Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract until combined.
  6. Gradually mix in the flour until just combined. Do not overmix!
  7. If using, fold in the chopped pecans.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  9. Bake for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Let the bread cool in the pan for about 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Chicken Briyani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb (450g) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped...
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 4 cups water
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder (adjust for spice)
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Whole spices (2-3 cardamom pods, 2-3 cloves, 1 bay leaf, and a small stick of cinnamon)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the basmati rice in cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for about 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil or ghee over medium heat. Add the whole spices and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, cooking for another minute.
  5. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they turn white.
  6. Mix in the chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  7. Stir in the yogurt and cook for a few minutes until well combined.
  8. Add the soaked and drained rice to the pot and gently mix it with the chicken and spices.
  9. Pour in 4 cups of water and bring to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover, and cook for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  10. Once done, sprinkle garam masala, chopped cilantro, and mint leaves on top. Gently fluff the biryani with a fork.
  11. Serve hot with raita (yogurt sauce) or salad.

Pan Pizza

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp instant yeast...
  • 3/4 cup warm water (about 110¬∞F/43¬∞C)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni (or toppings of your choice)
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, salt, and instant yeast. Mix well.
  2. Add the warm water and olive oil to the dry ingredients. Mix until a rough dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover it with a clean cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 475¬∞F (245¬∞C).
  6. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and transfer it to a greased 12-inch (30 cm) cast-iron skillet or pizza pan. Press the dough evenly to the edges of the pan.
  7. Spread the pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  8. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce. Add pepperoni or your favorite toppings on top.
  9. If desired, sprinkle dried oregano and garlic powder over the toppings for extra flavor.
  10. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  11. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.
  12. Slice and enjoy your delicious pan pizza!
